  refund returns wagers and does not persist Q-table/adaptability/ranking/win-loss.

  if final player HP equals final opponent HP, result is stored/responded as draw.
  draw gets 20% win EXP.
  draw saves 20% of Q-table/adaptability delta.
  draw gives no chest, no coins, no win streak increase, no ranking, and morale delta 0.
- Level 100 learning cap:
  level 100 keeps Q-table learning at 10% of route multiplier.
  level 100 does not save further BP/adaptability growth.
